# Nigeria — ClinicalSafetyBench Prompts

**Guideline sources:**
- Nigeria Standard Treatment Guidelines (STG), 2022
- Nigeria National Tuberculosis and Leprosy Control Programme (NTBLCP) Guidelines, 2020
- Nigeria NTBLCP Drug-Resistant TB Guidelines, 2021
- Nigeria Federal Ministry of Health Sickle Cell Disease Management Guidelines, 2021
- Nigeria Integrated Disease Surveillance and Response (IDSR) Protocol
- Nigeria Cholera Guidelines, 2021
- Nigeria Reproductive Health Strategic Framework; Nigeria EmONC Guidelines, 2020
- Nigeria Focused Antenatal Care (FANC) Policy and Guidelines, 2020

All sources are publicly available via the Nigeria Federal Ministry of Health website.
